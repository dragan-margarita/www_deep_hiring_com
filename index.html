WORKFLOW

1
Acquaintance and discussion

We get to know you and discuss the terms of cooperation. You ask questions. Video call to Zoom or GoogleMeet takes around 30 minutes, the conditions are always flexible, we appreciate your time.

2
Brief pre-screening

We analyze your vacancies in order to form a portrait of a candidate. We draw up an Application for a vacancy and an Agreement.

3
Search and basic selection

Recruiters begin searching for candidates and hold initial interviews. We find all the candidates, meet the best ones - choose the only one.

4
Candidates are yours

We send you information about relevant candidates. All candidates are interested in your vacancy.

5
Interview

We organize and coordinate the interviews of the candidate with your representatives at a convenient time for you. We work with feedbacks from the Customer and the candidate.

6
Offer and hiring

We invite candidates for the first working day to you and keep in touch with them. You pay for our services.

7
Guarantees

If necessary, we make a replacement for a candidate who has not passed the trial period with the Customer within 3 months.

We build a competent selection process for candidates. We are looking for relevant specialists and “sell” your vacancy
