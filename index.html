WORKFLOW

1 Acquaintance and discussion

We get to know you and discuss the terms of cooperation. You ask questions. It takes around 10 minutes, the conditions are always flexible, we appreciate your time.

2Brief pre-screening

We analyze your vacancies in order to form a portrait of a candidate.

3Search and basic selection

Recruiters begin searching for candidates and initial interviews. We find all the candidates, meet the best ones - choose the only one.

4Candidates are yours

We send you information about relevant candidates. All candidates are interested in your vacancy.

5Interview

We organize and coordinate the interviews of the candidate with your representatives at a convenient time for you. We work with feedbacks from the Customer and the candidate.

6Offer and hiring

We invite candidates for the first working day with you and keep in touch with them. You pay for our services.

7Guarantees

If necessary, we make a replacement for a candidate who has not passed the trial period with the Customer within 3 months.
